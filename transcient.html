WHO CAN PARTICIPATE

The Competition is open to candidates in pairs who
(a) are resident of India
(b) have no police charge against them.

EXAM PATTERN

Phase I: Screening Test
It will have multiple choice questions.

  • No. of questions: 40
  • Duration: 40 minutes

For each right answer 2.5 marks will be awarded and 0.5 marks will be deducted for wrong answer.

Phase II: Verbal Round
It will consist of ten rounds .

  • 1. History
  • 2. Political Science
  • 3. Geography
  • 4. Mythology
  • 5. Sports
  • 6. Physics
  • 7. Chemistry
  • 8. Music Round
  • 9. Current Affairs
  • 10. Snap round
  • 11. Rapid fire round

In every round for each right answer ten points will be awarded and no points will be deducted for wrong answer upto 2nd last round. Incase a team doesn't know the answer, it may pass to another team. For right answer of passed question five point would be awarded to next team.

* Full details about Verbal round will be updated after screening test.

* Rules are subjected to changes. Aspirants are advised to check website regularly.

SELECTION PROCESS

Phase I: Screening Test

All the eligible candidates have to undergo a screening test on the specified date, time and venue. E-call letter will be issued to candidates email Id.
